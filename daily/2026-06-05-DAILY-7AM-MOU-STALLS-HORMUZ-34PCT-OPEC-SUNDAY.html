🛢️ Story 2 — Hormuz Day 94: Physical Reality vs Market Price

The Hormuz July 31 market at 34.5% says there's a one-in-three chance Hormuz shipping returns to normal by end of July. The physical data says this is optimistic: 10 transits/day vs. 95/day pre-crisis, 300 vessels idling at Gulf ports, Brent crude ~$98/bbl, DHL projecting 4–6 months minimum to normalize.
