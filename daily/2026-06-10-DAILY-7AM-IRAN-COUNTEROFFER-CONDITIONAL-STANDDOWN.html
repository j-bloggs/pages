  • June 7-8: worst Iran-Israel strikes since April 21 ceasefire. Iran fired ~30 ballistic missiles toward US bases in Kuwait/Bahrain; Israel struck Tehran, Tabriz, Isfahan (Karun petrochemical partially damaged).
